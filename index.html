Introduction

Welcome to CrowdX, the next-generation crowdfunding platform powered by blockchain.

Blockchain Transparency

Trust Matters. Transparency is Key.

How It Works

A Quick Overview

1. Campaign Creation: Project creators list their ideas with transparent goals, milestones, and funding targets.

2. Crypto Contributions: Backers fund the campaign using major cryptocurrencies, ensuring fast and low-fee transactions.

3. Milestone-based Payouts: Funds are held in escrow and released to creators only when key milestones are met, building trust and accountability on both sides.
